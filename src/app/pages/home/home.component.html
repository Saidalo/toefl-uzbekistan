who we are

Welcome to the TOEFL Test Center in Uzbekistan, proudly serving as the authorized test center of ETS GLOBAL, the world's leading authority in English language assessments! We are absolutely thrilled to be your trusted partner on your journey to TOEFL success. With our team of dedicated professionals, we are committed to providing you with unparalleled guidance and support every step of the way. Whether your goal is to study abroad, advance your career, or pursue a master's or PhD, the TOEFL Test Center in Uzbekistan is your ultimate destination for turning your dreams into reality.
